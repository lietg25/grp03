Who We Are

The AniMall Pet Hotel & Shop is the Philippines' one of the biggest wholesale and retail stores of animal products carrying quality brands – from animal food, vitamins, accessories, and grooming products.


The company started in 2022 as a small pet shop to sell quality freshwater fish to Filipino hobbyists. The product line had expanded to include several products used in wholesale and retail such as dog and cat foods and accessories.


On September 03,2021, we launched our online store to cater to growing customers so they can order their pet products for home delivery.


Because we are a specialist pet care business, everything we sell, we ensure that we only sell from quality brands that have been tested to give our customers peace of mind.

Core Values

Integrity




We do what we say we'll do. We are dedicated to providing a great service, and that means we value trust and honesty and we give our 100% to providing the highest level of service by doing the right thing.
We’re also honest with each other as colleagues and hold each other to an account which makes for a great place to work that’s open and transparent.

Customers First




At ThePetshop.ph, we pride ourselves on our high level of customer care. We offer the best price without compromising the quality and make each transaction experience
easy, safe, and accessible to online buyers to ensure that we build loyalty and a good relationship with our customers.

Commitment to Excellence

Because pets are at the heart of everything we do, we believe that everyone in our company has a special responsibility to help keep pets happy, healthy, and safe. We are committed to giving our best to exceed our clients' expectations and adhere to
higher welfare standards to ensure that your pets are properly looked after

Mission Statement




To enrich the lives of pets and their families by providing peace of mind, trust, and security through our high-quality and value-for-money animal products. Our goal is to connect passionate people, trusted brands, and committed retailers to bring pets the very best.
